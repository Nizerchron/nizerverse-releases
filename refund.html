Refund Policy

We want you to be happy with every purchase on Nizervers.

Last updated: March 11, 2025
We offer a 7-day refund window for all paid theme purchases. If you are not satisfied, contact us within 7 days of your purchase and we will process your refund.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request a refund within 7 days of the original purchase date
  • The theme did not work as described or was not compatible with your device
  • You experienced a technical issue that we were unable to resolve
  • You were accidentally charged (duplicate purchase)

2. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

  • More than 7 days have passed since the purchase date
  • The theme has been downloaded and used without any reported issues
  • Refund is requested simply due to a change of mind after full use
  • The issue is caused by an incompatible third-party app not related to Nizervers

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at nizercreate@gmail.com with the following details:

  • Your full name and email address used for purchase
  • The name of the theme purchased
  • Your order or transaction ID (if available)
  • A brief reason for the refund request

We will review your request and respond within 3 business days.

4. Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed back to the original payment method. Processing time may take 5–10 business days depending on your bank or payment provider. Refunds are handled through our payment processor, Paddle.

5. Subscription Products

If Nizervers offers any subscription-based plans in the future, you may cancel at any time. Cancellations will take effect at the end of the current billing period. We do not provide prorated refunds for unused subscription time unless required by law.

6. Contact Us

For any questions about our refund policy or to initiate a refund, please contact us at nizercreate@gmail.com. We aim to resolve all issues fairly and promptly.
